## üé® App Icon Requirements

Your 1024x1024 icon should:
- Use your orange brand color (#F97316)
- Include a recognizable liquor/inventory symbol
- Work well at small sizes (display properly at 60x60, 40x40)
- No text (icon should be symbolic only)
- RGB color space
- No transparency
- No rounded corners (Apple adds these automatically)
